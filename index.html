Cyclone Fengal causes severe flooding in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, with over 200 homes submerged in Puducherry and significant disruptions to transport and daily life. The NDRF and Army are assisting in rescue operations.

Kerala battles ongoing floods and landslides, with 106 people confirmed dead and hundreds missing. Rescue efforts continue in affected areas amidst severe infrastructure damage.

Chennai struggles with the aftermath of extensive flooding five days after heavy rains, causing massive disruptions in transport and daily life across the city and nearby areas.

Karnataka braces for heavy rainfall with potential flash floods as a result of Cyclone Fengal's remnants, affecting South Interior and Coastal Karnataka. Local authorities are on high alert for possible flooding.

Puducherry faces critical flooding after the Sankaraparani River overflowed, affecting over 200 homes. The situation remains critical with ongoing evacuations and support from the Indian Army.
